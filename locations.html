Hakone

Home to seven local hot springs known as "The Seven Soup". The hot springs are rich in trace elements that are said to be beneficial to the human body.

Sapporo

Home to Sapporo Art Park, one of the country's most extraordinary outdoor institutions for contemporary sculptures. Sapporo is also home to the Sapporo Snow Festival, an annual weeklong event featuring snow and ice sculptures

Hiroshima

A city that's a must-see for understanding Japan's 20th-century history. Hiroshima is home to the Atomic Bomb Dome, the Peace Memorial Museum and Park, and a thriving city with art galleries, castles, and traditional gardens.

Kyoto

Considered the cultural capital of Japan, Kyoto is home to over 1,500 Buddhist temples, 400 Shinto shrines, and 17 UNESCO World Heritage sites.
